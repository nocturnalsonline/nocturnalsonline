Lifebuoy Defender

Problem

Nowadays most youth aren’t always aware of the virus, Some don’t wash their hands and take care of their health. They are not invincible therefore, this virus could put you in hospital for weeks or even kill you. So they should have the experience to learn how to protect themselves and defend against the virus.

Solution

Create an AR game app that allows young ones to use smartphone to trigger the lifebuoy product  that has an AR character that cleans up the gamified dirty hand, while playing they are also able to wash the hand. This will allow them to know more about the virus and have the experience to clean their selves for themselves and for others.
