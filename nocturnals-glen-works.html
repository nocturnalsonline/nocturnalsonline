Visioncare

Problem:

Many people have no knowledge on how to care for their eyes as we are abusing it unintentionally from our devices everyday and also, eye doctors are still using traditional ways to treat eye condition.

Solution:

Creating an app to help people with eye condition by doing eye exercise in a VR Box in a fun way. An eye tracking device will provide knowledge on how to care for their eyes and set a 20-20-20 rule break to prevent user from looking at the device for too long.
